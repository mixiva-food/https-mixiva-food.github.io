These classic waffles are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. They are easy to make, perfect for beginners, and ideal for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and add milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  5. Mix gently until just combined. Do not overmix.
  6. Lightly grease the waffle iron.
  7. Pour batter into the waffle iron and close the lid.
  8. Cook until the waffles are golden and crispy.
  9. Serve warm with syrup, honey, or fresh fruit.
